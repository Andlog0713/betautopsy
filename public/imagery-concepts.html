CONCEPT 01 // Y-INCISION BEHAVIORAL MAP

The three arms = three scores. Junction = overall grade.

Each arm of the Y represents a behavioral dimension. Color shows health (green=good, red=bad). The junction point is the overall grade.

CONCEPT 02 // BANKROLL DAMAGE REPORT

Your bankroll as a body. Injuries marked where leaks are.

A vertical "bankroll bar" with wound markers showing exactly where money is bleeding out. Each marker shows the leak, its severity, and the dollar cost.

CONCEPT 03 // BEHAVIORAL EKG

Your betting behavior as a heartbeat. Spikes = emotional events.

An EKG-style line that represents behavioral health over a session or time period. Normal rhythm = disciplined betting. Spikes = emotional events (loss chasing, stake escalation). Flatline = stopped betting (good or bad).
